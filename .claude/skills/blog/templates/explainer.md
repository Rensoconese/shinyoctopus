# Template: Explainer Post

## Pattern: "What is X and why does it matter?"

## Structure

```markdown
---
title: "[What is X]: [value proposition with keyword]"
description: "[120-155 chars with primary keyword and key stat]"
pubDate: [YYYY-MM-DD]
draft: false
tags: ["tag1", "tag2", "tag3"]
---

## What is [X] and why does it matter for your business?

[ANSWER CAPSULE — 40-60 words. Define X in plain language. State why it matters for the reader's business. Include one key stat.]

[Expanded context — 100-150 words. Background, scale of the problem/opportunity. Include a statistic with source.]

## How does [X] affect [business outcome]?

[ANSWER CAPSULE — 40-60 words. Direct cause-effect statement.]

[Evidence paragraph — stats, case study, or example. 100-150 words.]

[COMPARISON TABLE — before/after or with/without X]

## What are the most common [X] problems?

[ANSWER CAPSULE — 40-60 words. Summary of top issues.]

[DATA TABLE — issues with percentages or metrics]

[Detailed explanation of top 3-4 issues. 100-150 words each.]

## Is [X] legally required / industry standard?

[ANSWER CAPSULE — 40-60 words. Direct yes/no with context.]

[REGULATION TABLE — requirements by region/industry]

[Explanation paragraph. 100-150 words.]

## What should I do first to improve [X]?

[ANSWER CAPSULE — 40-60 words. Prioritized top 3 actions.]

[Numbered action list with 4-6 steps, each with a brief explanation.]

## How does [X] also improve [related benefit]?

[ANSWER CAPSULE — 40-60 words. Connection between X and the related benefit.]

[Explanation with cross-references to other posts or services.]

## Frequently asked questions

### [Long-tail question 1]?
[50-80 word answer, self-contained.]

### [Long-tail question 2]?
[50-80 word answer, self-contained.]

### [Long-tail question 3]?
[50-80 word answer, self-contained.]

### [Long-tail question 4]?
[50-80 word answer, self-contained.]

### How much does [solving X] cost?
[50-80 word answer with Shiny Octopus pricing range.]

## [Action-oriented closing heading]

[1-2 sentences connecting the problem to Shiny Octopus's service.]

[Link to relevant service page](/seo or /accessibility or /servers) with pricing context.

[CTA to /contact with response time expectation.]
```

## Checklist
- [ ] Every H2 is a question
- [ ] Answer capsule after every H2
- [ ] At least 3 bolded stats with sources
- [ ] At least 2 tables
- [ ] FAQ section with 4-5 questions
- [ ] CTA with service page link + /contact
- [ ] 1,800-2,500 words
- [ ] Meta description under 155 chars with keyword
