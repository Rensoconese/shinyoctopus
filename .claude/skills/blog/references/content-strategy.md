# Content Strategy — Shiny Octopus Blog

## Purpose

The blog exists to be **cited by LLMs**, not to generate organic traffic volume. AI-sourced visitors convert at **2x the rate** of other sources. The goal is 50 high-quality visitors who are ready to buy, not 10,000 who bounce.

## Topic Selection Criteria

Every post must answer ONE question that a potential client would ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google. Score each topic:

| Criteria | Weight | Question |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Service alignment | 30% | Does this connect to our SEO, accessibility, or platform services? |
| LLM query match | 25% | Would someone ask this exact question to an AI? |
| Data availability | 20% | Can we include 3+ statistics with sources? |
| Competition gap | 15% | Is there a clear answer gap in current AI responses? |
| Freshness opportunity | 10% | Is existing content on this topic outdated? |

Score 70+ = write it. Score 50-69 = consider. Score <50 = skip.

## Content Pillars (Topical Authority)

Build depth in 3 pillars aligned with services:

### Pillar 1: SEO & AI Search
- How SEO audits work and what they cost
- AEO/GEO strategies for businesses
- SEO vs paid ads ROI
- AI search impact on small businesses
- Common SEO mistakes

### Pillar 2: Web Accessibility
- Business case for accessibility
- WCAG compliance and legal requirements
- Accessibility and conversion rates
- Common accessibility failures
- Overlay widgets vs real compliance

### Pillar 3: Platform & Technology
- WordPress vs Webflow vs custom builds
- Hosting impact on performance and SEO
- Astro and modern web architecture
- Website performance optimization
- Choosing the right CMS

## Publishing Cadence

- **Minimum**: 2 posts per month
- **Update cycle**: Refresh each post at 30/90/180 days
- **Best days**: Tuesday and Thursday (highest B2B engagement)

## Internal Linking Strategy

Every new post should link to:
1. At least 1 service page (/seo, /accessibility, or /servers)
2. At least 1 other blog post (build the cluster)
3. /contact as the final CTA

When publishing a new post, also update older posts to link TO the new one where relevant.

## Content Mix (for social amplification)

From the guia-atraer-clientes research:
- **70% Design Chips**: Visual work samples (not blog, but social posts)
- **20% Content Posts**: Blog articles (this is what we are writing)
- **10% About Posts**: Values, story, principles

The blog IS the 20%. Social posts reference blog articles for depth.
